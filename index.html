HammerSpin Help

What is HammerSpin?

A fun, digital replacement for the classic pre-game coin toss. Decide who gets the hammer and who picks the rock colors with a quick tap or a satisfying swipe.

How to Use

Swipe Mode — Drag and flick the mascot to spin it manually. The harder you swipe, the longer it spins!

Tap Mode — Simply tap the mascot to initiate an automatic, randomized 3D coin flip.
